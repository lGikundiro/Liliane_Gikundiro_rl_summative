# RL Summative Report Checklist

- Problem and mission context (non-generic)
- Action space (exhaustive and relevant)
- Observation space definition
- Reward design with rationale
- Start state and terminal conditions
- Environment visualization evidence (GUI screenshots)
- Random-action simulation evidence
- Agent-in-environment diagram with labels
- DQN training and tuning results
- REINFORCE training and tuning results
- PPO training and tuning results
- A2C training and tuning results
- At least 10 hyperparameter runs per algorithm
- Comparative analysis across all methods
- Best model behavior discussion
- Video walkthrough evidence
